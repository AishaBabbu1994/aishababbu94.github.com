📚 About This Tool

Arabic to Latin Converter enables you to easily convert Arabic to Latin characters. The Latin output is the phonetic reading of the Arabic input text based on the ISO 233 standard.

ISO 233 establishes a system for romanization of Arabic script, designed to be comprehensive and reversible for consistent cataloging, indexing, and data exchange.
